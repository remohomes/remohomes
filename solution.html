Our Cost Structure is Better

With offsite construction using prefab components, one can train unskilled workers in the manufacturing trade, which leads to less dependence on hard-to-find construction workers. One can save on soft costs with standard designs and pre-approved plans. Since we plan to have the product speak for itself, we hope to minimize the marketing and overhead costs too.

Transportation costs are included in the cost of materials numbers are based on industry research and first-hand experience in the construction industry.

Faster Lead Times

Our modular construction process allows for simultaneous onsite and offsite work, significantly accelerating project timelines. With a goal of producing thousands of homes annually, we recognize that onsite substructure work (site preparation, foundation, utilities) is the primary constraint. Once modules are delivered, we complete homes in weeks rather than months.

Superior Quality

Inspired by the aerospace and semiconductor industries, our modular construction approach ensures the highest quality standards. Traditional construction materials are often vulnerable to damage due to onsite storage and exposure to the elements. In contrast, modular construction enables secure storage of materials, protecting them from moisture and other hazards. This not only saves money but also guarantees the longevity of the finished product.

Our commitment to quality extends to utilizing pre-cut and prefabricated materials with tight tolerances, resulting in an impeccable fit and finish. By surpassing traditional building code enforcement, we prioritize compliance and our materials’ integrity.

Decarbonization and Industrialization of Construction

Inspired by advanced manufacturing industries, our approach to construction is driven by decarbonization and industrialization. We aim to revolutionize the industry by addressing housing affordability and environmental concerns. Recent changes in zoning laws, such as the approval of accessory dwelling units (ADUs), allow the prefab manufacturers to support the increase in housing density and tackle affordability issues. The Biden Administration’s new housing plan further supports prefab homes by expanding financing options and streamlining approvals and construction processes.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

We are dedicated to creating a more sustainable construction industry by minimizing waste and reducing CO2 emissions. According to the UN Environment Program, buildings (construction and operations) are responsible for 40% of the world’s CO2 emissions. Our innovative construction methods, inspired by the aerospace and semiconductor industries, allow us to design for disassembly. This significantly reduces our environmental footprint while providing affordable and sustainable housing solutions.

We are not just building homes but instead taking a holistic approach required to solve the biggest problems in the world: climate change & housing affordability.
