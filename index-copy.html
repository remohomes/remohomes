About Us

Let Us Help You Find The Home Of Your Dreams.

We are pleased to present our very first ReMo Home in all of its glory. This new oasis is yours for the taking, complete with designer-level finishes and high ceilings. Upscale stainless steel appliances and luxury flooring add a contemporary element to your space. Enjoy your easily accessible rooftop made to host your housewarming party or enjoy your morning brew. This models skylight makes for a brighter home including marble countertops in your kitchen, and charming details such as the incorporation of a highly insulated and air-tight structural system on top of central air conditioning to keep you comfortable and save energy year-round. Light streams all throughout both bedrooms and throughout the halls through floor-to-ceiling windows making your home the most vibrant living space.

We wanted to give every family the unique opportunity to live spaciously by including a separate laundry room containing a washer and dryer coupled with designer tile in both bathrooms. We think you'll love it here.

ReBuild 2022

ReBuild 2022 was an invite-only leadership conclave focused on housing affordability, Sustainability and human-centered construction and design. We brought together policymakers, Non-profits, developers, technologists, and others that share our desire to make affordable, sustainable housing available to the masses. With opening remarks from LA Mayor Eric Garcetti and attendees from the Milken Institute, the CA Growth Council, and the California Secretary of Housing office, ReBuild gathered world-class thought leaders in a way that’s never been done before in the industry.
